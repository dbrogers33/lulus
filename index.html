About LULU's

Family owned and operated since 1932, Crass Furniture, INC. adopted the name LuLu's as the latest generation took over. LuLu's is known as one of Kentucky's most interesting furniture and home accessory resources.

We are a destination spot for travelers, collectors, bargain hunters, and folks who enjoy creating a unique home or business. Shoppers will find something interesting for everyone, from the ordinary to the extraordinary.

We hope that you can stop in soon and let us show you art, craftmanship, and the pure beauty of home decor.
